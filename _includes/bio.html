Short Bio

I am a cryptographer working as an Engineering Manager at DFINITY. Previously, I was a researcher in the Cryptology and Cryptographic Security group at IAIK, Graz University of Technology. My main research interests are in the field of provably secure public key cryptography, with a focus on cryptographically addressing security and privacy issues in modern computing scenarios (e.g., cloud computing or Blockchains). This also includes work on primitives, conjectured to remain secure in the advent of sufficiently powerful quantum computers. I hold a PhD degree (Dr.techn.) in computer science from Graz University of Technology, which I have obtained in 2017 with distinction.

I am a co-designer of the Picnic family of post-quantum digital signature schemes. Picnic is a third-round candidate in the post-quantum crypto project by NIST. For more information, news, and updates refer to this or to this page.
